The company is a private limited liability company and according to the following provisions shall have effects:- The right to transfer shares is restricted in the manner herein prescribed. The numbers of members of the company (exclusive of persons who are in the employment of the company and for person who having been formerly in the employment and have continued to be members of the company) limited and provided that where two or more persons hold one or more shares in the company jointly shall for the purpose of this Articles be treated as a single member. The company shall not have power to issue share warrant to bearer. The invitation of the public to subscribe for any shares or debentures of the company is prohibited.

TRANSFER AND TRANSMISSION OF SHARES.

Any part off this agreement proposing to transfer any shares shall give notice in writing to other parties. The transfer notice shall specify the number of shares, the transfer or proposes to transfer. The initial parties to this agreement shall have priority over any other party to purchase such share.

Subject to the restrictions of this articles as may be applicable, any member may transfer all or any of his share by instrument in writing in any usual or common form or any other form which the directors may approved.

The directors may decline to register the transfer of a share not being fully paid to a person of whom they shall not approve and they may also decline to register the transfer of the share on which the company has a lien. The directors will also be empowered to cancel any share or shares of any person and refund that person on the value of the share. The directors will not be required to give any reason for their actions.

The directors may also decline to authorize any instruction transfer unless. A fee such as the directors may from time to time require is paid to the company in thereof.

The instrument of transfer is accompanied by the certificate of the share to which it relates and such other evidence as the directors may be reasonably required to show the right on the transfer to make the transfer, and.
The transfer or has first given to the initial shareholders of the company an option to purchase the shares in the respect of which the transfer is made. If the directors refuse to register the transfer, they shall within two months after the date on which the transfer was lodged with the company sent to the transferee notice of the refusal.

The registration of transfer may suspended and the register closed such times and for such periods as the directors may from time determine provided always that such registration shall not be suspended for.

The company shall be entitled to charge a fee on the registration of a transfer or any probate, letters of administration, and certificate of death or marriage, power of attorney notice in lieu of distrait as or other instrument affecting the title to any share.

In the case of death of a member or survivor(s) if the deceased was a joint holder and legal person representative of the deceased where he was a sole holder shall be the only person recognized by the company as having any title to his interest in the share but nothing herein contained shall release the estate of a deceased joint holder from any liability in respect of any share which has been jointly held by the holder or other persons.

In case of death of the majority shareholders, his shares shall be managed by the administrators of the deceased estate for the deceased’s beneficiaries.

CONVERSIONS OF SHARES INTO STOCK.

The company may by ordinary resolution convert any paid up share into stock and reconvert any stock into paid up shares of any denomination.

The holders of stock may transfer the sale or any part thereof in the same manner and subject to the same regulations as and subject to which the share from which the stock arose might previous to conservation have been transferred or and near thereto as circumstances admit and the directors may from time to time fix the minimum amount of stock transferable provided that such minimum shall not exceed the nominal amount of the shares which the stock arose.

The holders of stock shall according to the amount of stock held by them have the same rights, privileges and advantages as regards dividend voting at the meeting of the company and other matters as if the held the shares from which the stock arose but no such privilege or advantage (except participation in the dividend and profits of the company and the assets on a winding up) shall be conferred that privilege or advantage.

Such articles of the company as are applicable to paid up shares shall apply to stock and the words‘ and’ shareholders ‘therein shall include’ share and shareholder’.

INCREASE OF CAPITAL (ALTERNATIVE OF CAPITAL)

The company may from time to time by ordinary resolution increase the share capital by such sum to be divided into shares of such amount as resolution shall prescribe.

The company may by ordinary resolution before the issue any new shares determining that the same or any of them shall be offered in the first instance and either expert at premium to hold the existing holders of any classes of shares in proportion as a nearly as may be to the amount of the capital held by them executively or make any other provision as to the issues of the new shares; but in default of any such determination or so far the same shall not extend new shares may be dealt with as if they formed part of the share in the original capital.

Except so far as otherwise provided by the conditions of issues or by this articles any capital raised by the creation of new shares shall be considered part of the original capital and shall be subject to the provision herein contained with reference to the payment of the calls and installments, transfer and transmission, forfeiture, lien, surrender and otherwise, unless otherwise provided in accordance with those articles, the new shares shall be ordinary shares.

The company may from time to time by ordinary resolution:
Consolidate and divide all or any of its share capital into shares of large amount than its existing shares.
Subdivide its shares or any of them into shares or smaller amount than its fixed by its memorandum of association subject nevertheless to the provisions of section 63(1) (d) of the act and so that the resolution whereby any shares are subdivided may determine that as between the resulting shares one or such shares may be given any preference or advantage as regards capital, voting or otherwise over the others or any other of such shares; or.
Cancel any share which at the date of the passing of the resolution have not been taken or agreed to be taken by any person and diminish the amount of the share capital by the amount of shares so called.

GENERAL MEETING

The company shall in each year hold a general meeting at its annual general meeting in addition to any other meeting that year, and shall specify the meeting as such in the notice calling it and more than fifteen month of the incorporation, but it needs not in the year of its corporation in the following year. The annual general meeting shall be held at such time place, as directors shall decide.

The company nay by special resolution reduce its capital redemption, reserved fund or premium account in any matter and with subject to any indecent authorized and consent required by the law. All meetings other than general meetings shall be called extraordinary general meetings.

The directors may whenever think fit, convene at an extra ordinary general meetings shall also be convened on such requisition or in default may be convened as provided by section 123 of the act. If at any time there are not within Uganda sufficient directors capable of acting to form a quorum, any directors or two members of the company may convened an extraordinary general meeting in the same manner or as nearly as possible as that in which meetings may be convened by the directors.

NOTICE OF GENERAL MEETING.

A general meeting shall be by 21days’ notice in writing at least. The notice shall be exclusive of the day on which its served or deemed to be served and notices from the company shall not withstanding that it is called by short notice than specified in this article be deemed to have been duly called if it is so agreed. In the case of a meeting called as the annual general meeting by all the members entitled to attend and vote thereat.

In the case of any other meetings by a majority in numbers of the members having right to attend and vote the meeting being a majority together holding not less than 95 percent in nominal values of the shares giving that right.

The accidental omission to give notice of a meeting to a non-received of notice of a meeting by any person entitled to receive notice not invalidate the proceedings at that meeting.

PROCEEDINGS AT GENERAL MEETING.

All business shall be deemed special if transacted at an extraordinary meeting and also all business which is transacted at an annual general meeting with the exception of declaring a dividend, the consideration of the accounts, balance sheets and the reports of the directors, and Auditors, the election of the directors in place of those retiring and the appointment fixing of the remuneration of the Auditors.

No business shall be transacted at any general meeting unless a quorum of members is present at the time when the meeting proceed to business, save as herein otherwise provided two members present in person all be a quorum.

If within half an hour from the time appointed for the meeting a quorum is not present, a meeting if convened upon the requisition of members shall be dissolved; in any case it shall stand adjourned to the same day and in the next week at the time and place to such other day and at the meeting a quorum is not present within half an hour from the time appointed for the meeting the members present shall be a quorum.

The chairperson or any of the board of directors shall preside as chairperson at every general meeting of the company or if there is not such chairperson or if he or she cannot be present within fifteen minutes after the time appointed for the holding of the meeting or if unwilling to act the directors present shall elect one of their members to be chairperson of the meeting.

If any meeting directors is willing to act as chairperson or no director is present within fifteen minutes as time appointed from holding the meeting with the members. When a meeting is adjourned for fifteen days or more than seven clear days’ notice, a list of the adjourned meeting shall be given an original meeting but it shall not be necessary to be transacted at the adjourned meeting.

At a general meeting a resolution put to the vote of the meeting shall be decided by a show of hands unless a poll(before or on the declaration of the show of hands) is demanded:
By the chairperson of the meeting or; By a member or members holding shares in the company conferring a right to vote at the meeting being shares on which an aggregate sum has been paid upon all the shares conferring that right.

Unless a poll be so demanded the declaration by the chairperson of the meeting that a resolution has by a show of hands been carried or carried unanimously or by a particular majority or loss and entry to that effect in the book containing the minutes of the proceeding or the company shall by conclusive evidence of facts without proof of the numbers of proportion of the vote recorded in favor of or against such resolution.

In the case of an equality of votes whether by show of hands or on a poll chairperson of the meeting at which show of hand takes place or at which the poll is demanded shall be entitle to second or casting votes.

Printed copy of every special resolution and other resolutions or agreement mentioned in section 143 of the act be sent to the register or companies as provided by that section.

VOTES OF MEMBERS.

Subject to any rights or restrictions for the time being attached to any class or classes of shares on a show of hands, every members present in person shall have one vote and poll every member shall vote according to the number of shares he has.

DIRECTORS.

The number of directors and the names of the first directors shall be determined in writing by the subscribers of the memorandum of the association or a majority of them and until such determination, the signatories to the memorandum of the association shall be first directors.

The remuneration of the directors shall from time to time be determined by the company in a general meeting. Such remuneration shall be deemed on accrue from day to day. The directors may also be paid all travelling, hotel, and other expenses properly incurred by the attending or retiring from meeting of the company or in connection in any business of the company.

Any director who serve on any committee or devotes special attention to the business of the company or otherwise perform services, which in the option from the directors are outside the scope of the ordinary duties of directors or otherwise as the board of directors may determine.

BORROWING POWERS.

The directors may exercise all the power of the company to borrow money and to mortgage or charge its undertaking, property uncalled capital or any part thereof and to issue debenture stock whether out-right or as security for any debt liability or obligations of the company or any third party. The directors are hereby empowered to borrow up to one hundred million Uganda shillings, (100 million) or its equivalent at any one time on behalf of the company.

The business of the company shall be managed by the directors who paid all expenses incurred in promoting and registering the company and may exercise all such power of the company as are not by the act or by those articles required to be exercised by the company in general meeting subject, nevertheless to such regulation of or provision as may be described by the company at general meeting. But no regulation made by the company in general meeting shall invalidate any prior act of directors, which would have been valid, if that regulation had not been made.

The directors may from time to time and at any time by power of attorneys appoint any company, firm or person or body of person whether nominated directly by directors to the attorney or attorneys of the company of just the discretion (not exceeding those vested exercisable by directors under those articles) and for such period and such subject to such condition as they think fit, and any such powers of attorneys may contain such provision for the protection and convenience of person dealing with such attorney and may also authorize such authorities and discretion vested in him.

MANAGING DIRECTORS.

The directors may entrust to confer up on a managing director any of the power exercised by such terms and conditions and with such restriction as they think fit and whether collaterally with or the exclusion of their own power from time revoke, alter, or all or any such powers, the directors shall from time to time appoint one of them to the office of the managing directors of the company shall be vested in him/her.
